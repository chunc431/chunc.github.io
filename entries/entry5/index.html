"Someday Long After" is part of a poem collection by Kim So-Wol. His collection revolves around the sorrow of separation. The poem is written as one long imaginary dialogue between two separated lovers. "Someday Long After" is one of the few poems in So-Wol's collection that has a more softer, calmer, atmosphere. This is because despite the separation, the lover is still alive, leaving some hope for a reunion in the future.
